## Neighborhood Map

This map starts you on the Sheraton New Orleans Hotel. The sidewalk along Canal Street is up, and across Canal Street, further up is the Marriott. There are crosswalks to your left and right to cross Canal Street, and there is a tram in the middle of Canal Street. Camp Street is left and Magazine Street is right. Press ctrl+arrow keys or the Next forward, next left, and other next commands to jump to the next object in that direction. Press “m” or “Features menu” for all objects. Select filters in the Features Menu and press enter or double-tap to select and unselect different feature types, then tab or swipe to the apply button. Press “o” or “Orient to Feature” on sidewalks or roads to make them strait in front of you, and press “shift+down arrow” or “Rotate 180 degrees” to face the other direction.

## Marriott Hotel Map

This map starts you with the hotel up from your location. The elevators and escalators are left, the check-in desk is to the right, and the lounge and 555 Restaurant is directly up. Press “m” or “Features menu” for all objects and “shift+m” or “Nearby features” for only objects on the floor. Press “l” or “Change building level” to switch levels.

This map only has levels 1-3, and does not include the following rooms:

-   4th floor: Balcony L, Balcony K, Bonaparte.
-   5th floor: Beaurgard, Galvez, Audubon.
-   41st floor: Riverview 1 and 2, St. Charles and Lafayette.

## Sheraton Hotel Map

This map starts you with the hotel down from your location. Starbucks, the elevators, escalators, and Fedex are left, Pelican Bar, and parking garage are to the right, and the front-desk and gift-shop are strait down. The exhibit hall is on the 3rd floor. Press “m” or “Features menu” for all objects and “shift+m” or “Nearby features” for only objects on the floor. Press “l” or “Change building level” to switch levels. Press “space” on exhibit booths to read their description.
