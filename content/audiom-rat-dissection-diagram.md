## Diagram 1: Rat Ventral View

This diagram presents a ventral view of the abdominal cavity. It shows the positioning and external layout of major digestive organs, including the diaphragm, liver, stomach, spleen, small intestine, and caecum. It provides an overall view of how the organs are arranged within the abdominal cavity from underneath.

## Diagram 2: Open Abdominal Cavity

This diagram offers a detailed view of the internal structures within the abdominal cavity after dissection. It displays organs such as the liver, stomach, spleen, pancreas, duodenum, jejunum, ileum, caecum, and colon. This view allows for examination of the digestive system and its connected structures in more depth.
