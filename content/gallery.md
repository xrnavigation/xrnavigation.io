# Audiom Gallery

Below are a range of different diagrams and maps we have represented with Audiom. There are two general map categories: Referential maps, where the features themselves are the most important aspect (e.g., neighborhood, building, and campus maps); and thematic, where there’s a numeric or categorical element overlaid on geographic features (e.g., COVID statistics, eclipse, election results, etc.) We also have spatial diagrams that are not maps.

## Tutorial Intro Demo

If you haven’t experienced Audiom before, please first complete [the interactive Audiom tutorial (Opens in a new tab)](https://xrnavigation.io/magicmap-paloalto/).

## **Thematic**

### COVID Statistic Map

[This COVID-19 statistic choropleth map is over the U.S. (Opens in a new tab). It was the first accessible digital disease map ever created.](https://xrnavigation.io/audiom-covid-map/)

### Eclipse 2024

[This eclipse 2024 isopleth map shows lines of totality over the U.S. (Opens in a new tab).](https://xrnavigation.io/eclipse24/)

### 2024 Election Result Map

[Here is a snapshot from the 2024 U.S. national election (Opens a new tab)](https://xrnavigation.io/election2024/). This was the first ever accessible live election result map.

### Sea-Level Rise Map

[This is a mean sea-level rise map (Opens a new tab)](https://xrnavigation.io/audiom-mean-sea-level-rise-map-fall-2024/). This shows ocean bowies that indicate the percentage above or below the global mean sea-level rise that is at each location. This also has all the countries in the world, so could be used as a globe map.

## **Spatial Diagrams**

### Human Skeleton Diagram

[Here is an interactive human skeleton diagram (Opens a new tab)](https://xrnavigation.io/audiom-human-skeleton-diagram/). It is the only fully labeled accessible digital human skeleton diagram. It has a default step size that allows viewing the smallest finger bones, but press “=” 7-10 times and the step-size will become larger.
